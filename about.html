Meet the revolutionary new way to explore the web. Avocado utilizes data from global web trends and from your browsing history to map the "genome" of the internet, based on real, useful characteristics like subject matter and average user retention rate. Our unobtrusive plugin then uses this data to present you with interesting new websites that you'll wish you would have heard of sooner. Enjoy our special guacamole of the web, made just for you.
